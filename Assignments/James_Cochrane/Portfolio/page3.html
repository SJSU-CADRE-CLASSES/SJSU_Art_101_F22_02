"Untitled", 870p x 870p, Adobe Illustrator

I made this piece with the intention of depicting a carefree and happy scene. I struggle with anxiety and neuroticism, and I often feel like an alien. One of the times I feel most at ease is when I am with fellow aliens, driving around, and listening to cool music. I always love seeing animals and talking to friendly strangers, too. So I made something to commemorate those enjoyable and almost taken-for-granted moments.
